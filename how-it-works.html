How Matched Betting Works

Matched betting is a strategy that allows you to profit from free bet promotions offered by bookmakers. By placing a bet on both an outcome and its opposite using a betting exchange, you can cover all results and secure a risk-free return.

Here’s how:

  1. Sign Up: Choose a bookmaker with a sign-up offer.
  2. Place a Qualifying Bet: Bet your own money on a result using the bookmaker and lay it on an exchange.
  3. Get a Free Bet: Once your qualifying bet settles, the bookmaker gives you a free bet.
  4. Extract Profit: Use our calculator to stake your free bet in a way that guarantees a profit no matter the result.

It's 100% legal, tax-free in the UK, and completely based on maths — no gambling involved.
