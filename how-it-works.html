How Matched Betting Works

Matched betting is a legal, risk-free way to turn bookmaker promotions into real profit by betting on all outcomes of an event using two accounts: a bookmaker and a betting exchange.

Step-by-Step

  1. Sign up for a bookmaker offer – e.g. Bet £10, get £30 in free bets
  2. Place a qualifying bet on the bookmaker
  3. Lay the same bet (the opposite outcome) on a betting exchange
  4. Receive your free bet from the bookmaker
  5. Repeat the process with the free bet, guaranteeing profit

By using odds calculators and following exact steps, you're never gambling — you're exploiting maths and offers. We'll show you how.
