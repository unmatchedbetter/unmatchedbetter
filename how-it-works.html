How Matched Betting Works

1. Find an Offer

Bookmakers frequently run promotions like "Bet £10, Get £30 in Free Bets". These offers are designed to attract new customers.

2. Place a Qualifying Bet

Use our calculator to place a back bet with the bookmaker and a lay bet on a betting exchange. This ensures you neither win nor lose, and you unlock the free bet.

3. Use the Free Bet

Place another set of back and lay bets with your free bet stake. Because the free bet cost you nothing, you lock in a guaranteed profit regardless of the outcome.

4. Track Your Profits

Use the Expense Tracker to log your bets, profits, and bookmaker activity. This keeps you organised and helps you spot your most profitable offers.
