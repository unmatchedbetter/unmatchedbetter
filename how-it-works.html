How Matched Betting Works

Matched betting is a method that allows you to turn bookmaker free bets and promotions into real money with minimal risk. Here's how it works:

  • Step 1: Sign up to a bookmaker offering a free bet or welcome offer.
  • Step 2: Place a back bet (betting for an outcome) on the bookmaker's site.
  • Step 3: Place a lay bet (betting against the same outcome) on a betting exchange.
  • Step 4: Use our calculator to ensure your lay stake and liability are correct so you lock in a minimal loss.
  • Step 5: Once the back bet settles, use the free bet and repeat the matched betting process to lock in a profit.

It may seem confusing at first, but our guides and calculators make it easy to follow. Best of all, it's completely legal and tax-free in the UK.
