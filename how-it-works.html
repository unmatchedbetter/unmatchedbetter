How It Works

Matched betting is a method that allows you to profit from free bet promotions offered by bookmakers. By placing two opposing bets (a back bet and a lay bet), you eliminate the risk and lock in a profit.

Here’s how it works in simple steps:

  1. Find a free bet offer – Visit our Offers page to see available signup bonuses.
  2. Place a qualifying bet – Use the Calculator to find the correct stakes for your qualifying bet.
  3. Receive a free bet – Once your qualifying bet settles, the bookmaker gives you a free bet.
  4. Place your free bet – Again use the calculator to determine the lay stake for your free bet and lock in profit regardless of the outcome.

We’re here to make this process as easy and risk-free as possible with tools and guides built for you.
