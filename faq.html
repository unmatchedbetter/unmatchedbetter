Frequently Asked Questions

What is matched betting?

Matched betting is a way to profit from bookmaker offers by placing opposing bets on all outcomes of an event to guarantee a return.

Is matched betting legal?

Yes, matched betting is completely legal in the UK and does not involve any form of gambling.

Do I need to pay tax on my profits?

No, gambling winnings in the UK are tax-free, including those from matched betting.

What do I need to get started?

You'll need a computer or smartphone, internet connection, and accounts with online bookmakers and betting exchanges.

What is a lay bet?

A lay bet is a bet against an outcome, typically placed on a betting exchange like Betfair or Smarkets.

How does your calculator work?

Our calculator helps you determine the correct lay stake and expected profit or loss for both qualifying and free bets.

Why do I need to sign up for an account?

Creating an account allows you to access tools like our expenses tracker, so you can track profits and losses.
